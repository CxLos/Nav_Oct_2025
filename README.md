## üìù Description 

An interactive Plotly/Dash dashboard that tracks and visualizes client navigation and care coordination activities at the Black Men's Health Clinic (BMHC). This report provides comprehensive insights into patient navigation services, resource connection success rates, care coordination efforts, and client support metrics. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor navigation effectiveness, identify barriers to care access, and make data-driven decisions to improve client support services and healthcare system navigation.

## üß™ Methodology

The data for this project was sourced directly from the Black Men's Health Clinic

## Plans for collected data:

- Track client navigation success rates to identify effective support strategies
- Analyze resource connection patterns to optimize service delivery
- Monitor care coordination effectiveness and client satisfaction
- Generate automated navigation reports for care teams and stakeholders
- Identify common barriers to healthcare access and develop solutions

## üîç Findings

### Client Navigation Services

* Total clients coordinated for medical services
* Types of navigation services provided (insurance, appointments, transportation, etc.)
* Client navigation success rates and completion metrics

### Resource Connection

* Healthcare resources and services connected (specialty care, mental health, social services, etc.)
* Time to resource connection and appointment scheduling
* Follow-up care coordination and client retention rates

### Barriers to Care

* Common obstacles identified (insurance, transportation, language, trust, etc.)
* Solutions implemented and their effectiveness
* Client demographic patterns requiring targeted support

### Navigation Impact

* Client health outcomes and service utilization improvements
* Client satisfaction with navigation services
* Reduction in missed appointments and care gaps

## ‚úÖ Conclusion

The Client Navigation and Care Coordination Report provides valuable insights into the effectiveness of BMHC's patient support and healthcare system navigation services. The data collected enables leadership to make informed decisions about navigation strategies, resource allocation, and care coordination improvements. By tracking client success metrics and identifying barriers to care, this dashboard supports continuous improvement in helping clients access needed healthcare services. Regular monitoring of these metrics helps ensure that navigation services effectively break down systemic barriers, connect Black men with essential health resources, and improve overall health outcomes. This data-driven approach continues to advance BMHC's mission of ensuring equitable access to quality healthcare and addressing social determinants of health.
